# 📘 Introduction to Digital Forensics Certified Course 
### Course Registration Guide

Welcome! Follow the steps below to register and start your course.

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Sign Up
1. Go to 👉 https://www.talentlms.com/  
2. Create a new account  
3. Verify your email address  
4. Log in to your account  

---

## 👤 Step 2: Switch to Learner
1. Click on your **profile icon** (top-right corner)  
2. Change your role/status to **Learner**

---

## 📚 Step 3: Enroll in the Course
1. Open the course link 👉  
   https://forensiqally.talentlms.com/plus/catalog/courses/126  
2. Click **Sign Up / Enroll**

---

## ✅ Step 4: Start Learning
- Once enrolled, you can begin the course immediately 🎉  

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes
- Make sure you are **logged in** before accessing the course link  
- If the course doesn’t appear, try:
  - Refreshing the page  
  - Logging out and logging back in  

---

## 💡 Need Help?
If you face any issues during registration, try repeating the steps or checking your email verification.

---

✨ Happy Learning!
